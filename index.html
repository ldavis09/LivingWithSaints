SAFE MILITARY HOUSING INITIATIVE, 501C3

Safe Military Housing Initiative seeks to provide the military-affiliated population access to safe and healthy installation housing by facilitating oversight and accountability through the education of stakeholders.

ABOUT US

Our Focus

Our members and volunteers provide the momentum that helps us affect change. Using grassroots advocacy, we provide solutions that make a long-lasting difference for military families and service members alike.

Our Vision

We aim to be the leading organization providing solutions to the military-affiliated population by improving access to safe and healthy housing while protecting tenants' rights at military installations.

Our Team

Seeing a need for proactive advocacy on behalf of military families our team of volunteers formed this nonprofit to help service members and their families ensure they are in safe and healthy homes.
